The idea

A standard tour shows “important points”. I am interested in places that speak quietly: courtyards, staircases, traces of German Breslau, post-war resettlement, murals, local bars and moments where the city reveals its true structure.

🏚 Hidden places

Unexpected routes and micro-histories.

🎙 Gonzo narrative

The author’s subjective perspective.

☕ Local rhythm

Coffee, conversations and everyday life.

What happens during the walk?

We do not run from point to point. We read the city like a living text: architecture, art, historical traces and the way people inhabit space.
